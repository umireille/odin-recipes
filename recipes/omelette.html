Description

Masala omelette made with curry powder,garam masala and green chilli

ingredients

  • eggs
  • garam masala
  • green chilli
  • tomato
  • spring onions
  • vegetable oil
  • fresh coriander
  • curry powder
  • garlic clove
    •

    Steps

    1. To make masala omelette break two eggs in a bowl.
    2. Add chopped onions, tomatoes, green chillies, salt and whisk it well to form a foamy well-beaten egg batter.
    3. Heat a cast-iron skillet or non-stick pan. I prefer a cast-iron skillet for making omelettes for the perfect texture and easy handling.
    4. Add ghee/butter or oil and allow to heat for a few seconds.
    5. Pour prepared egg batter into the skillet and cook for 2 minutes on medium heat.
    6. Drizzle some oil on the sides of the omelete in the skillet and flip it to cook from the other side as well.
    7. Indian masala omelette is ready.
